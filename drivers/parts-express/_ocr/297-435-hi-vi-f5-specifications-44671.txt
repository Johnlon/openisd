F5 Bass-Midrange Features:

— Light and extremely rigid cone made from
Keviar®/paper composite

— Specially made high-loss rubber surround

— Shielded double magnet motor structure

— High power handling Kapton® former voice coil

— Central phase plug

— High density aluminum die-cast basket

The design of the F5 has been optimized for an extend-

ed and dynamic bass reproduction in small vented sys-

tems. Midrange clarity and accuracy is remarkable.

The F5 utilizes a newly developed matrix of Keviar® and

paper fibers. As a result, the cone weighs less, is more

rigid, and has an improved dampening factor over con-

The massive aluminum die-cast basket has been devel-

A shielded magnet structure allows the F5 to be easily
incorporated into audio/video applications. The driver
may be used in a small closed box as a bass-midrange
unit when a subwoofer is employed.
