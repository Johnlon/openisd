and paper fibers. As a result, the cone weighs less, is

hand coated with a special dampening compound to fur-

low through a wide frequency and power range.

sitic structural resonance.

sion and ensure maximum power handling. Together
with the long-throw design, this feature provides clean
and articulated bass performance at high volume levels.
